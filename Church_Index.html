Our Mission

Learn | Grow | Serve

At Kingsport First we have a passion for you learn and experience God's redeeming love, grow in community with others and serve your neighbors.

Our mission is simple, we want you to learn, grow, and serve.

Learn

Attend and participate in service on Sunday morning.

Grow

Connect and get involved in a local growth group.

Serve

Use your gifts to give back to the church and your community.

Our Ministries

There's Something For Everyone

Kids

Each Sunday morning we offer classes for kids of every age at 9am. Also, during service at 10am we have K1 Kids located upstairs.

Students

We have classes on Sunday mornings for students middle through high school. Also, on Wensday nights at 7pm we have K1 Fusion which is for middle school through high school.

Adults

For adults we offer classes on various topics on Sunday morning at 9am. We also have lifegroups that adults can connect with.
